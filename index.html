❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking a shift?

Open the app, tap "Start Shift", select your service (Uber, DoorDash, Lyft, etc.), enter your starting odometer reading, and hit Start. It's that easy!

How do I remove ads?

Go to Settings → Premium section and purchase "Remove Ads" for a one-time payment. Enjoy an ad-free experience forever!

Can I export my shift data?

Yes! Go to the Dashboard and use the export feature to download your complete shift history.

What delivery services are supported?

We support Uber, Uber Eats, Lyft, DoorDash, Instacart, Grubhub, Postmates, Amazon Flex, and more!

How accurate is the mileage tracking?

Mileage is calculated based on your starting and ending odometer readings, ensuring accurate tracking for tax deductions and earnings analysis.
